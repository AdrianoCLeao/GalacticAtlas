About the
Project

Galactic Atlas is a pioneering project dedicated to advanced exoplanet identification using innovative artificial intelligence techniques. Developed to explore and gain deeper insights into the universe beyond our solar system.

01. Project Vision and Inspiration:

Galactic Atlas is fueled by a profound passion for astrophysics and an insatiable curiosity about extraterrestrial life. Inspired by the quest to understand our universe's mysteries, this project aims to push the boundaries of exoplanet discovery using innovative AI technologies.

02. Development Approach and Methodologies:

Galactic Atlas employs a rigorous approach integrating advanced AI methodologies tailored for exoplanetary research. Leveraging machine learning algorithms such as K-Nearest Neighbors (KNN) and Random Forest, the project analyzes intricate patterns in stellar data to detect and characterize exoplanets. Additionally, signal processing techniques and statistical models enhance the accuracy and reliability of planetary identifications.

Galactic Atlas aims to make a significant impact on exoplanetary research, paving the way for new discoveries and insights into the universe's diversity.
